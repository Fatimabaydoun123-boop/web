Our Signature Appetizers

Welcome to our Appetizers Menu! Before diving into our exquisite Lebanese-Japanese fusion dishes, start your journy with a selection of handcrafted appetizers designed to awaken your taste buds.

We take pride in offering a variety of savory bites that blend the bold flavors of Lebanon with the delicate artistry of Japanese cuisine. From crispy, golden delights to fresh and vibrant small plates, each appetizer is crafted to set the perfect tone for your dining experience.

Let every bite transport you from the bustling souks of Beirut to the tranquil sushi bars of Tokyo, where tradition meets innovation in every dish.
